Group processes

Overall, our group worked well together as we communicated efficiently and effectively. In Assignment 2 we had frequent group calls every week varying between 1-2. We discussed progression of tasks and helped each other if some tasks were taking longer than usual to complete. I believe that our communication skills were quite high tier as there was constant communication on different types of media, we used messenger groups and teams mainly to interact and share thoughts. We worked very well as a team, getting work done and not getting distracted in meetings. We shared work throughout the group and contribution was shared out as evenly as we could.

We all gelled very well together pretty much from the start with everyone talking in meetings and in the messenger group sharing ideas and thoughts. Each member was very supportive of other members if there were any times where something came up and the member could not attend a meeting or anything like that. Each individual team member gave updates on what they were up to in the work and estimates on completion times which made the timeline of work easily trackable.
